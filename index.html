Japanese Cooking Classes

Learn to Cook Japanese

Introductory to Expert Cooking Classes in Traditional and Modern Japanese Cooking Techniques. Single Classes are offered daily, such as Introduction to Japanese Sauces 101. Courses lasting up to 4 weeks, such as Sashimi/Sushi and Sauces ~ Masterclass, are offered as well. Experience, first-hand, the many nuances of this fascinating oriental culture.
