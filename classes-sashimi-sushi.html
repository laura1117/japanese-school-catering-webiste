Japanese Sashimi & Sushi


  • Author | Yoko's Kitchen

A four week dive into Japanese Sashimi and Sushi. It's all about the fish!.

Sushi and Sashimi is delicious and fun to make!

While you may associate Japanese cuisine with Kobe Beef and seafood, many Japanese staples are actually already vegan! This includes rice, seaweed, mushrooms, tofu, sesame seeds, and certain vegetables!
