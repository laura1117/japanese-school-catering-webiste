Placing Catering Orders

Yoko's Kitchen accepts small catering orders, i.e. lunch, small parties, via Email. A return email is required to confirm order. A phone call requesting payment will follow within a half-hour of email confirmation. Online Ordering is Coming Soon! Make sure to place the correct details for your order and we will get back to you shortly. In case we didn’t get in touch, give us a call (727) 000-0000.

Please read the following:
  1. We only accept orders with a minimum total price of $200.
  2. The form doesn’t serve as a confirmation. Please wait for our call that would confirm your catering order.
  3. Order at least 3 days before your event excluding weekends. It is recommended to order earlier to give us time to order the fish for your event.
  4. Take note that any kind of fish order can be substituted to another one depending on the availability. Get in touch with us for more information.
  5. Delivering only within 5 mile radius from the restaurant location.

Thank you for your business and, please, come back soon.
